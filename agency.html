Why SZU

4 resons to choose Shenzhen University

SZ city of innovation

Shenzhen is a young, modern and one of the fastest developing cities in China, world’s leading global technology hub known as China’s Silicon Valley. It hosts the Shenzhen Stock Exchange as well as the headquarters of numerous multinational companies such as Tencent, ZTE, Huawei, DJI, BYD, Vanke, SF Express, JXD, Hytera, CIMC, Shenzhen Airlines, Ping An Bank, Ping An Insurance and China Merchants Bank. Shenzhen ranks 14th in the 2019 Global Financial Centers Index. It has the third busiest container port in the world.

Shenzhen is the city of innovation. The first stock in China was issued in Shenzhen, and the first land auction in the country was held in Shenzhen as well. Since the city became a special economic zone, it has played a role as an innovator to spread new ideas and a pioneer to explore the path of reform and opening up in China.

SZU Ranking

As Shenzhen continues developing with rapid speed so does SZU. Nowadays, it is regarded as the fastest developing university in China, and also one of the “Top 100 Universities in China”. A famous alumnus is Ma Huateng, the founder and CEO of Chinese internet giant Tencent. The University now occupying the #63 position among Chinese Universities and #240 among Asian Universities. Of course, it’s not among top 10 yet however, during the past decade SZU has shown tremendous progress so the important point here which has to be highlighted not the current position but it’s rate of growth which was outstanding and steady for the mentioned time-frame. So, one can easily predict where it is going to be in the nearest future if it can keep the same growth rate. The remaining question is who will be able to identify this undervalued asset now.

Admission requirements

Among other advantages that SZU offers to foreign students, one is that administration of university only requires a few documents for admission. That is potential students should demonstrate basic competence in English listening, reading and writing and must hold high school degree or diploma.

But this situation will not remain long, as University’s ranking grows fast there are more and more international students applying each year. Due to that there will be competition for fixed number of places and University administration is going to pursue new policies for admission. So, for those who wish to study in a very good university but cannot afford it because of some reasons SZU might be good destination but them need to take fast decision in order to not miss this an opportunity.

Scolarship

There are several sources of scholarship opportunities for new students as well as for outstanding students. One of such sources is provided by the local Government in order to attract and encourage more outstanding foreign students to study in Guangdong, vigorously promote the health and rapid development of international students studying in Guangdong Province, raise the national influence and internationalization level of higher education in Guangdong, in 2013, the Government of Guangdong Province set up the "Governmental Scholarship Program for international students in Guangdong".

The other famous source is provided by University itself in order to improve the university’s international competitiveness, accelerate the university’s globalization process and process toward becoming a high-level university, attract more international students, and motivate current international students, the university hereby founds awards and scholarships for the international students of Shenzhen University, in the spirit of Plan for the Internationalization of Shenzhen University (2016-2020) as well as plan for the Cultural Innovation and Development of Shenzhen University.

No other University of the same level can offer the same level of opportunity as SZU does.
